Privacy Policy

At Homelab Diagnosis, your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal and health information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details (name, contact info, address) provided during booking or registration.
  • Health and test-related information required for diagnostics.
  • Payment details for processing transactions (we do not store card/UPI details).
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and usage statistics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process your bookings and deliver lab services.
  • To communicate with you about appointments, reports, and support.
  • To improve our services and website experience.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Data Security

  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.
  • Access to your information is restricted to authorized personnel only.

4. Sharing of Information

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.
  • Information may be shared with partner labs and service providers only as needed to fulfill your requests.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

5. Cookies & Tracking

  • We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site usage.
  • You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time.
  • Contact us if you have questions or concerns about your privacy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact us.
